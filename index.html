About

I graduated college from Brigham Young University with a bachelors degree in Exercise Science. My original plan was to go to Occupational Therapy school. After scoring well on the GRE I started applying to some schools around the country and even a university in Australia. Shortly after my wife and I found out the she was pregnant. We decided to stay where we were to be around family and that's when I started working at a software company in Draper named IDS as an account manager.

IDS built a web application for mortgage companies where they can input the information for a loan and generate data dynamic documents. As an account manager I managed some of the top accounts and made customizations for them by writing custom code in documents, adjusting the code in calculation wizards, and coding custom loan programs. This made me want to get into web development and become a web developer or computer programmer. I initially started learning on my own but decided to apply to Lambda School to make sure that I was learning industry best practices and building a good base.
